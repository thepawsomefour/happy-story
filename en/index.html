Chapter 1
A holiday, a name, and a feeling

It was a warm summer holiday in Italy. The family soaked up the sunshine, the food, the carefree feeling of being away — and every evening there was music, laughter, and an entertainer who made everyone smile. His name was Happy.

☀️

Not because that was his official name. But because he simply was that way — radiant, warm-hearted, always in good spirits. Everyone who met Happy couldn't help but smile. The name stuck. In their minds. In their hearts. Without knowing it, he had left the family a gift — a name that would one day be needed.

After a few beautiful weeks, the family packed their bags and headed home. Life was waiting. School was starting again. The everyday routine returned.

What the family didn't know: the most beautiful part was still ahead of them.

Chapter 2
The notice on the wall

It started in the most unspectacular way. No big drama. No grand plan. Just a notice — pinned to the wall in the staircase, between letterboxes and doorbells.

⚠️ Emergency Kittens — Hand-raised

Rescued from a farm. Found in very poor condition. Malnourished, had fleas and a severe eye infection. Treated with antibiotics, dewormed, defleaed. Now eating independently. Looking for loving homes.

Next to the text: three small photos. Three tiny kittens who had already seen the rougher side of life. Found on a farm. Abandoned. Sick. But rescued — by people who hadn't looked away.

Mum saw the notice. Saw the photos. And one of those photos simply wouldn't let her go.

Number 2. A small grey-and-white tabby kitten. A little boy, around seven weeks old. He looked directly into the camera — with eyes that were curious and just a little lost at the same time. As if he was saying: "Hello. I'm waiting here."

Chapter 3
The message that changed everything

The message was written quickly. Short, but heartfelt. Could they come by and meet the kittens? The answer came promptly: Yes. Of course.

What followed was the most wonderful kind of chaos. The family prepared for their first kitten as if a little royal was on their way — which, in a way, was exactly right.

Lea vacuumed the entire flat until every last speck of dust had surrendered. The litter tray was set up with military precision. Food, bowls, a cosy little bed — everything was ready. The kitten was going to arrive like a long-awaited guest.

Max couldn't sit still. He wandered through the flat, checked the clock every few minutes, and asked at least three times: "When are we finally leaving?"

Dad smiled quietly. Mum had looked at the photo one more time. And all four of them got into the car — together, as always, for the important things in life.

✦ ✦ ✦
🥹
Chapter 4
Even cuter than in the photo

When they arrived and saw the little grey-and-white kitten in real life for the first time, it was over for all of them. Immediately. Without discussion.

The photo had already made him look adorable. But in real life? In real life he was even cuter. His tiny face. His big eyes. That soft grey-and-white fur that felt like velvet when you touched him.

He snuggled up to them straight away, as if to say: "Yes. You're the right ones. I'm coming with you." No hesitation. No hiding. Just that warm, quiet trust — as if he had always known that this family was coming for him.

Lea could barely stop stroking him. Max beamed without saying a word. Dad held him briefly in his hands and was lost. And Mum? Mum knew in exactly that moment: he belongs with us.

They drove home. The kitten sat on Mum's lap — small, warm, purring. And as the road passed by outside, the family began to ask themselves a very important question:

"What shall we call him?"
✦ ✦ ✦
✨
Chapter 5
A name that was simply meant to be

Naming a pet can sometimes be complicated. You think. You discard. You make lists. You argue gently about whose suggestion is better.

This time it was different.

Someone mentioned the holiday. The entertainer. The man who had made everyone shine every single evening — simply because he was so full of joy, so full of warmth, so full of life. The man whose name was Happy.

And then everyone looked at the tiny kitten on Mum's lap — this little creature who had already given them all so much happiness in just a few hours. Who had already made their hearts warmer than they had ever been before.

From this day forward, his name is
Happy

Because he makes everyone happy — simply by being there. Because no other name would ever have sounded so right. Because some names just wait until the right one comes along.

✦ ✦ ✦

Chapter 6
Home — from the very first second

When Happy entered the flat, he did so like someone who had never lived anywhere else. He sniffed briefly. Looked around. And then — off he went.

Every corner was inspected. Every piece of furniture sniffed. Every rug tested. Happy explored his new home with a curiosity that held not a trace of fear — as if he had simply finally arrived where he was always meant to be.

That evening, when the family sat together playing a board game — cards on the table, laughter in the air — Happy simply settled in with them. Right in the middle. As if that was his place. As if he had always known: when the people are together, I am there too.

He slept with the children. He cuddled on Mum's lap. He sat nearby when Dad came home in the evenings. He was simply — there. For everyone. Always.

🐱
Happy

Grey-and-white. The heart of the family. The quiet comforter who simply feels when someone needs him — and is always right there.

💙 warm-hearted 🤗 cuddly ✨ curious 🏠 always there 💛 where it all began

Happy was the first. The first of something that would grow into something much bigger. He laid the foundation for a family that couldn't stop giving their hearts away — to fluffy, four-pawed, purring, meowing creatures who deserved it just as much as he did.

He didn't know that, of course. He was simply lying on the sofa, purring.

But sometimes the greatest beginnings are exactly like that — quiet, warm, and with eyes closed on a cosy cushion.

✦ ✦ ✦

And so it all began.

A notice on the staircase wall.
A tiny kitten found on a farm.
A family that looked — and chose to say yes.

Happy came first.
But he would not stay alone.
Because where a heart is this big, there is always room for more.
